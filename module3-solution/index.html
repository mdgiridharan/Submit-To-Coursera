We Serve,

Chicken

Our chicken dishes are prepared in traditional South Indian style with fresh spices and homely cooking methods. The chicken is marinated with ginger, garlic, chilli powder, turmeric and special house masala to bring strong and tasty flavour. Slowly cooked with curry leaves, onions and tomatoes. Whether it is spicy the warm taste of authentic South Indian kitchen.

Mutton

Our mutton items are cooked with classic South Indian spices which give a deep and bold flavour. The mutton is slow cooked with freshly ground pepper, coriander, fennel and curry leaves so that the meat becomes soft and full of taste. Prepared in thick masala gravy very popular in South Indian homes and traditional mess style restaurants.

Pork

South Indian style, using local spices and slow cooking method. The pork is marinated with chilli, pepper, turmeric and garlic, then cooked with curry leaves. The meat becomes tender and juicy while the masala gives a strong and satisfying taste. Each plate offers the homely flavour that reminds of village style cooking and traditional South Indian meals.
